Spinach Cheese

Ingredients per item:

  • Olive Oil
  • Fresh cream
  • Tomato paste
  • Finely chopped onions
  • 1 box of Spinach
  • Ginger and garlic paste
  • Some salt, turmeric, and red chilli powder

Steps To Make Butter Chicken

  • In a medium-sized skillet heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the onion paste and saut√© until it is brown and cooked throughout about 5 minutes.
  • Pour the tomato paste over the onion paste and let it simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add salt, 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric and 1-2 teaspoon of red chilli powder
  • Stir it for 2-3 minutes and then add 1/4 cup of cream(add according to your own preferences) and water
  • Let it cook for 10-15minutes and then add Farmers cheese into in and its ready to served.
